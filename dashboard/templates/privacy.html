ServerCore Privacy Policy

This page explains what information ServerCore processes, why that information is used, and how billing, dashboard, and server configuration data are handled.

Service ServerCore

1. Information we process

ServerCore may process Discord account identifiers, server identifiers, role identifiers, command permissions, support settings, moderation settings, logs, premium billing state, and other configuration data needed to operate the bot and dashboard.

2. Why this data is used

We use this information to provide core product features such as dashboard access, command restrictions, support ticket routing, logging, moderation tools, welcome and leave configuration, ServerGuard protections, and Premium subscription access.

3. Discord account and server data

When you sign in with Discord, ServerCore may process your Discord user ID, username, display name, and the list of servers your account can manage. Server settings are stored so your configuration continues to work across restarts, redeploys, and updates.

4. Billing data

Premium purchases are processed through Discord. ServerCore stores the Discord SKU entitlement details needed to tell whether a server has Premium active, which server the entitlement belongs to, and when the entitlement ends if Discord provides that timing. Payment card details are handled by Discord, not by ServerCore.

5. Data sharing

ServerCore does not sell personal data. Information may be shared with infrastructure and payment providers only to the extent needed to operate the product, process subscriptions, maintain uptime, or comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

ServerCore uses reasonable technical and operational safeguards to protect stored settings, dashboard sessions, and billing-linked account data. No internet-connected service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your responsibility

Server owners and administrators are responsible for the configuration choices they make in ServerCore, including moderation automations, role access, logging settings, support flows, and any content they choose to store through the service.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time as the product evolves. Continued use of ServerCore after updates are published means you accept the revised policy.
